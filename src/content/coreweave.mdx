### Executive Summary

CoreWeave is a specialized cloud computing platform that focuses on providing GPU-accelerated infrastructure for artificial intelligence, machine learning, and high-performance computing applications.

## GPU-First Architecture

CoreWeave's infrastructure is built around GPU computing, offering:

- **High-performance NVIDIA GPUs** including A100, H100, and RTX series
- **Kubernetes-native orchestration** for containerized workloads
- **Flexible pricing models** with spot and reserved instances
- **Direct GPU access** without virtualization overhead
